Why Gray Coding

Binary representations of interger values have a flaw in certain applications. If I am to compare the number 15 and 16 then in the decimal representation I should only notice a difference of 1. The binary representation shows four bits that need to flip. For a couple of machine learning applications, genetic algorithms as well as certain communication problems another binary method to store numbers might be better.
