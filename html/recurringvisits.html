I remember a time when growth hackers called themselves marketeers, it was around the same time when data scientists called themselves statisticians.

I've done a bit of growth hacking for some websites and I've explored the thought of what might be a good metric in a dashboard. It's hard to think of something that's not a vanity metric. There are many ways to measure usage of an app but it is hard to think of any one metric that could sum up progress the best. Instead of focussing on a metric, I've focussed on a plot.
